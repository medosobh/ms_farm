Farming and Agriculture Management

This module contain a management tool and organize farm productive and service activity to ensure and control expenditure and activities.

"Project" is the main object to control.

4 types of Project:

  1. Produce only: Controlling on produce process; added to stock all the way long.
  2. Produce and sell: Controlling on how much produce and how much sold.
  3. Sell only:Controlling on how much sold on project lifetime.
  4. Service:Controlling on service e.g(device, vehicle, Quality control, pest control, internal maintenance service etc. )


Order and Actual:

spending use 3 type of order (Operation - Material - Expenses) that create account/stock movements.


Produce and Sales:

Additional 2 types of orders balancing the spending


Service Bills and Invoices:

For Service Projects the balance of spending from internal activities (bills) and external activities (invoices to external parties)


Project Summary:

a comparison will be shown in summary page between planned and actual spent and time.

showing progress and comparison in ratio and progress widgets. like; actual vs budget - plan vs actual - initial gross profit/loss - time gone vs  time planed.
